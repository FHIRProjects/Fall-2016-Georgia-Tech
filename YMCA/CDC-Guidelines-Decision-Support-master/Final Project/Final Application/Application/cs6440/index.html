Welcome

The objective of the project is to simplify the zika(or other similar epidemic diseases) guidelines and create triggers based on those simplified guidelines to help the doctors or practitioners making an informed decision about a patient and how to treat the patient The triggers will be based on the zika guidelines for example: patient's travel to zika affected countries. This will help doctors identify such patients quickly saving valuable time in treatment and possible containment of the disease.
